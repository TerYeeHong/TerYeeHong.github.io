About

Genre : Shoot 'em up

Development time : 2 weeks

Team : 1 people

Software : Unity, PlayFab

Target Platform: PC


A school assignment to implement playfab for a space shooter game. Required features include Log in, Sign up, Password Reset, Leaderboard, Player Data, Virtual economy, User Inventory. Some out of scope topics that I learnt on my own is cloud-scripting / Server-API. On my own, I polished the game further by adding music and background that can be purchased through PlayFab.

Game synopsis

Crisis! Asteroids have been cluttering up the Galaxos Highway. It's your job to help clear these pesky rocks. Upgrade your ship while you are at it.

How to play

- WASD

- Space to shoot

- Q to leave level

- R to restart level

When asteroid is destroyed, you gain score. You lose when hit by asteroid. Asteroids move faster when you are higher level, this is because your boss sends you to a more dangerous location if you are experienced.

Hero Adventure

About

Genre : Topdown 2D Action RPG

Development time : 10 weeks

Team : 1 people

Software : Visual Studio

Target Platform: PC


a complete and polished 2D game made in OpenGL that was a school assignment. It consists of a player with 3 animations, 4 npcs, 4 weapons, 3 abilities, 2 AI enemies, 1 AI pet and 4 levels. Inventory system and UI is made using Imgui, Irrklang sound library for game bgm and sfx and other gameplay elements.

My main learning was implementing physics in a 2D game, using libraries such as Irrklang, making AI, implementing spritesheet animations, singletons managers.

Game synopsis

Bored of everyday life, you decide to go on an adventure around the world with your adorable pet fox. Solve puzzles, take down the angry cobra whose trying to bite you, nap suspicious coins that just so happens to be on the ground, make new friends to learn their fighting style.

How to play

- WASD

- Mouse click to shoot

- Q to use abilities

Walk to the door to win the level, ricochet your spears to hit triggers to clear obstacles. Talk to an npc and gain their ability and weapon to replace your spears.

About

Genre : Simulation

Development time : 4 weeks

Team : 1 people

Software : Visual Studio

Target Platform: PC


This project was a school assignment and also my first experience with 3D opengl. There was a lighting shader given to us, we just had to learn how to implement lighting into the scene. Things I learnt were Cubemaps for the scene Skybox, third person and first person camera control. Character/Camera control inspired by Genshin Impact, serializing obj, mtl and tga files and implementing the OpenGL render pipeline.

Game synopsis

It's getting dark, Grandpa wants you to pick up the lanterns that he hid in the chests. Get them back so you and Grandpa can see in the night. Be careful of suspicious strangers...

How to play

- WASD

- Mouse drag, scroll to look around, zoom

- E to pick up items

- Right click to drop items

Find 3 lanterns and bring back to the house without getting killed by the Hilichurl monsters. You can only pick up 1 item at a time.
