About

Genre : Shoot 'em up

Development time : 2 weeks

Team : 1 people

Software : Unity, PlayFab

Target Platform: PC


A school assignment to implement playfab for a space shooter game. Required features include Log in, Sign up, Password Reset, Leaderboard, Player Data, Virtual economy, User Inventory. Some out of scope topics that I learnt on my own is cloud-scripting / Server-API. On my own, I polished the game further by adding music and background that can be purchased through PlayFab.

Game synopsis

Crisis! Asteroids have been cluttering up the Galaxos Highway. It's your job to help clear these pesky rocks. Upgrade your ship while you are at it.

How to play

- WASD

- Space to shoot

- Q to leave level

- R to restart level

When asteroid is destroyed, you gain score. You lose when hit by asteroid. Asteroids move faster when you are higher level, this is because your boss sends you to a more dangerous location if you are experienced.
