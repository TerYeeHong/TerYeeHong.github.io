About

Genre : Tower Defense

Development time : 2 weeks

Team : 4 people

Software : Unity, Github

Target Platform: PC


I was the leader of the project and my contributions include the Main menu, integration/version control, base defence mode, main UIUX for the level and settings for the game, such as volume control. This was the first Unity project that I had to develop with other people.

From this project, my main learning is how to handle merge conflicts, doing version control and how to communicate and work together in a team as a programmer.

Game synopsis

Your space operation has just ended in a failure, now stranded in an alien planet with hostile creatures. Your only hope is to survive for as many days as possible until your crew finds and rescues you.

How to play

Use your robot dog to collect materials during the day. The dog has limited power before it runs out of battery. Use those materials to build and upgrade your base. Watch your base defence take out the enemies.

About

Genre : 2D Action Platformer

Development time : 5 weeks

Team : 1

Target Platform: PC


Originally a school assignment, I expanded the scope with my own game ideas to make it into a complete game. Game consists of character with 4 abilities, 7 unique enemies, 3 levels, 1 boss fight and other gameplay elements.

From this project, my main take away was making platformer movements for player including crouch, dash, double jump, rolling. Making enemy AI and using A-Star pathfinding for my enemies. Making a modular ability system using Scriptable objects that both player and enemies can use. Using Unity Events to reduce dependecies.

Game synopsis

A great evil has befall the land, The demon lord has risen. Defeat waves of enemies, bandits, demons, golems. Obtain blessings from your goddess. You must save the land by slaying the demon lord.

How To Play

- WASD & Space bar

- Mouse to aim and shoot

- QEC to cast abilities

Find Statues of your goddess to use as checkpoints and get stronger with blessings. Explore the level to find the portal to proceed to the next level.

About

Genre : First Person Shooter, Realistic, Psychological Horror

Development time : 2 weeks

Team : 1

Target Platform: PC


Originally a school assignment, I expanded the scope with my own game ideas to make it into a complete game. Game consists of FPS elements of 6 different weapons, grenades, mounted machine guns, 2 levels and other gameplay elements.

From this project, my main take away was making 3D movements for player including crouching, proning, sprinting. Making realistic effects such as explosions and muzzle flashes using particle systems. Making recoil and camera shake effects in the presence of explosions and gun fire. Using interfaces to make the code more modular. Applying the SOLID principles in coding.

Game synopsis

The princess is in danger, save the princess! Complete objectives of destroying enemy bases and taking down helicopters. Your arsenal consists of rifles, rocket launches, snipers and much more! You can even steal the gun from the enemies' hands.

How To Play

- WASD & Space bar

- Mouse to aim and shoot, Right click to ADS

- Sprint to move faster

- Ctrl to Crouch

- Alt to Prone

- Touch the beacon of light once you complete the objective to proceed.

About

Genre : 2D Action Isometric

Development time : 3 weeks

Team : 1

Target Platform: PC


Originally a school assignment, I expanded the scope with my own game ideas to make it into a complete game. Game consists 5 unique entities, Inventory system, healing from consuming items, 1 boss fight and 1 quest.

This was my first ever game made using Unity. With the help of tutorials and self learning on the web, I learnt most of the basics of game making in Unity which includes, Scriptable objects, Unity's Input Manager, Unity's Project settings, Sprite animations. Things that i self learn out of scope in this project are A-star pathfinding, Interactive Inventory UI, Quest system.

Game synopsis

Your pal lazy joe has fallen asleep, it's up to you to complete the mission of retrieving the stolen scrolls. Beat your way through waves of enemies but be careful, the fighting style your master taught you renders you unable to move when charging your attack.

How To Play

- WASD

- Space to attack

You cannot move while charging your attack, how will you defeat your enemies?
