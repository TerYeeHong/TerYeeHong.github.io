When free, I seek out Events and convention that could be beneficial to my game development journey, here are some of them!

Gamescon Asia 2023

This was a convention held at Singapore Suntec City where game developers show up to share exchanges with one another and play test games.

>

I informed this with some of my classmates to see the local games being developed by people of my age.

There, I was acquainted with a student from Singapore Polytechnic that was developing his own game.

Tokyo Game Show 2023

In September of 2023, I was lucky to be selected for a overseas trip with the course to visit the Tokyo Game Show.

In a 8 Day trip, I visited game companies such as Bandai Namco and Creek and River co.

Among the events, one of the highlights was the Tokyo Game Show, one of the largest game conventions annually.

There, I playtested some of the games and shared exchanges with the developers.

One of my biggest surprises was play testing a rhythm game, on a controller, I never thought that could be possible but it had proven me otherwise that a Rhythm game can indeed even be played on controllers.

Temasek Metaverse 2023

Held in Singapore Marina bays convention centre, I was lucky to be a helper for this event.

This metaverse event was organised by a foreign country to showcase their technology on Virtual Reality, VR.

There I got to handle VR games and tools, and was taught about the capabilites of VR.

I also got to playtest some VR Games, such as Beat Saber and Horror Immersive game.
